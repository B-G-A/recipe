Sausage Breakfast Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground sausage
  • 6 large eggs
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 6 slices of bread, cubed
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a skillet, cook sausage over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  4. Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish. Spread bread cubes in the dish.
  5. Top with cooked sausage and sprinkle cheese over it.
  6. Pour egg mixture evenly over the casserole.
  7. Cover and refrigerate overnight or at least 4 hours.
  8. Bake uncovered for 45–50 minutes, until the top is golden and the center is set.
